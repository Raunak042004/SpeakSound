About Speak & Shop

Speak & Shop is an innovative voice-powered shopping assistant that lets you search the web using your voice. Our goal is to make browsing and shopping easier, faster, and more accessible for everyone.

Using the latest in speech recognition technology, Speak & Shop listens to your voice commands and performs real-time Google searches without needing to type.

This project was created with passion by:

  • Mayank (12300965)
  • Akshit Gauttam (12307332)
  • Ishanth (12319044)

We hope Speak & Shop makes your online search experience smoother and more fun. Try it out by clicking the mic icon and speaking your search!
