Frequently Asked Questions

01. What is Speak & Shop?

Speak & Shop is a voice-powered shopping assistant that allows users to search for products and content by speaking, instead of typing.

02. How accurate is the voice recognition?

Our tool uses modern browser-based speech recognition APIs which are quite accurate in quiet environments. Accuracy may vary depending on device and pronunciation.

03. Does Speak & Shop perform real-time searches?

Yes! It performs real-time searches by converting your spoken command into a Google search instantly.

04. Do I need to install any extensions?

No installation is required. It works directly in supported modern browsers like Chrome and Edge.
